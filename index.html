About Us

Welcome to Vanillaz Second Hand Shop – where pre-loved treasures find a new home! We believe in giving fashion and everyday essentials a second chance while promoting sustainability in style.

From vintage apparel to rare accessories and home décor, our curated collection is full of hidden gems. Whether you're a bargain hunter, a vintage lover, or an eco-conscious shopper, there's something special waiting for you at Vanillaz.

Our Mission

At Vanillaz, our goal is simple: to make second-hand shopping an exciting, stylish, and eco-friendly experience. We believe in quality, authenticity, and giving back to the planet by reducing waste, one amazing find at a time.
